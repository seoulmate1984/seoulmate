  • The Regular Program at SeoulMate is designed for foreigners and overseas Koreans interested in learning the Korean language and culture. It is suitable for those planning to enter universities or graduate schools in Korea, seeking employment, or studying for leisure.
  • Student Demographics: An average of 1,700 students from around 60 countries enroll each semester.
  • Classes align with the levels of the Korean Language Proficiency Test (TOPIK). Starting from Level 1, it takes approximately 1 year and 6 months to complete all 6 levels up to Level 6.
  • Course Structure: The Regular program consists of a 10-week course per semester, totaling 200 hours, offered across 4 semesters annually.
  • Class Schedule:
    - Morning Course: Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM - 1:00 PM
    - Afternoon Course: Monday to Friday, 2:00 PM - 5:50 PM
  • Application Fee (Non-refundable): 100,000 KRW

  • Academic Regulations
      • Placement Test: Students will take the placement test online.
      • Class Cancellation: If a class has fewer than five students, it will be cancelled.
      • Advancement Requirement: Students must attend at least 80% of the total class hours to advance to the next level.
      • Failure Policy: If a student fails the same level twice, they will not be allowed to continue courses at Yonsei Korean Language Institute.
      • Format: The exam, orientation, and classes will all be conducted online.
      • Orientation: Orientation will be held on the first day of class, followed by real-time online lessons via Zoom.
  • Learning content and Textbooks

    • Level 1

      • Textbook: Yonsei Korean in 3 Weeks 1
      • You can fully learn Hangul and read and write.
      • You know the basic pronunciation system and can accurately pronounce the initial, medial, and final sounds.
      • You can understand and use the basic structure of the Korean language and tense expressions.
      • You have the language skills necessary for basic daily life, such as greetings, self-introduction, and ordering food.

      Level 2

      • Textbook: Yonsei Korean in 3 Weeks 2
      • You can understand and use the basic structure of the Korean language, tense expressions, and sentence connection methods.
      • You can understand and use descriptive forms, conjecture expressions, reason expressions, plan expressions, and sequential expressions.
      • You have the language skills necessary for basic daily life, such as shopping, finding directions, using public transportation, and making phone calls.

      Level 3

      • Textbook: Yonsei Korean in 3 Weeks 3
      • You can accurately pronounce the individual sounds and phonetic rules of the Korean language in sentences.
      • You can use vocabulary related to introductions, food, shopping, invitations, and transportation.
      • You can use connected sentences and indirect sentences based on the simple sentences learned in Level 2.
      • You can appropriately use honorifics and informal language according to the conversation situation.

      Level 4

      • Textbook: Yonsei Korean in 3 Weeks 4
      • You can accurately pronounce the individual sounds and phonetic rules of the Korean language in sentences.
      • You can use vocabulary related to public institutions, phones, hospitals, travel, and household chores.
      • You can use indirect speech appropriately according to the conversation situation.
      • You can solve various problems encountered in daily life to some extent with the knowledge gained.
      • You can understand the simple daily culture.

      Level 5

      • Textbook: Yonsei Korean in 3 Weeks 5
      • You develop communication skills that enable daily life in Korea.
      • Through speaking practice such as storytelling, you learn to express your opinions correctly.
      • You listen to and understand dialogues with relatively complex contexts and read texts that help understand Korean society and thought patterns, thereby expanding your vocabulary.
      • You express your thoughts in prose through writing practice, enhancing the accuracy of grammar and vocabulary usage.

      Level 6

      • Textbook: Yonsei Korean in 3 Weeks 6
      • Based on the basic grammar learned from Levels 1 to 5, you can make various expressions.
      • You expand your vocabulary not only with everyday terms but also with Sino-Korean vocabulary, gaining a deeper understanding of Korean thought patterns and culture.
      • You can engage in daily conversation and express your opinions in detail through longer narratives. You can also write texts on everyday topics.
      • You can listen to and understand relatively long dialogues and texts about Korean society and culture, enhancing your understanding of the content.

      Level 7

      • Textbook: Yonsei Korean in 3 Weeks 7
      • You cultivate the ability to understand academic and specialized texts in fields such as humanities, social sciences, and the arts, and to express your opinions logically.
      • You can understand news, newspaper articles, and current materials and express your opinions.
      • You understand Korean values related to language, education, work and leisure, economy, popular culture, tradition, family, and society.

      Level 8

      • Textbook: Yonsei Korean in 3 Weeks 8
      • You can freely use Korean grammar to engage in complex and specialized activities.
      • You learn vocabulary expansion and the thought patterns of Koreans through professional materials that reflect Korean society and culture.
      • You cultivate language skills that allow you to participate in university-level activities.
