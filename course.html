Regular course

  • The Regular Program at the Korean Language Institute is designed for foreigners and overseas Koreans interested in learning the Korean language and culture. It is suitable for those planning to enter universities or graduate schools in Korea, seeking employment, or studying for leisure.
  • Student Demographics: An average of 1,700 students from around 60 countries enroll each semester.
  • Classes: Align with the levels of the Korean Language Proficiency Test (TOPIK). Starting from Level 1, it takes approximately 1 year and 6 months to complete all 6 levels up to Level 6.
  • Course Structure: The Regular program consists of a 10-week course per semester, totaling 200 hours, offered across 4 semesters annually.
  • Class Schedule:
    • Morning Course: Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM - 1:00 PM
    • Afternoon Course: Monday to Friday, 2:00 PM - 5:50 PM
    • Weekday morning course: Mon, Tue, Thu, Fri, 6:00-7:00
    • Weekday evening course: Mon, Tue, Thu, Fri, 18:00-19:00
    • Weekend morning course: Sat, Sun, 10:00-12:00
    • Weekend afternoon course: Sat, Sun, 16:00-18:00
  • Application Fee: Non-refundable, 200rs
  • Tuition Fee (per semester): 1,800rs
